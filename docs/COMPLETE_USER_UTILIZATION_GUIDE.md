## A - Account Setup & Authentication

### **Getting Started**
1. **Visit the UATP Portal**: Go to `https://uatp.app` or download the mobile app
2. **Identity Verification**: Connect with:
   - Government ID (for maximum UBA eligibility)
   - Email verification (basic level)
   - Social media accounts (optional, for social attribution)
   - Biometric verification (advanced security)

### **Authentication Methods**
- **Password + 2FA**: Traditional login with SMS/authenticator
- **Wallet Connect**: Use crypto wallets (MetaMask, etc.)
- **Biometric**: Face ID, fingerprint, voice recognition
- **Hardware Keys**: YubiKey and similar devices

### **Account Types**
- **Personal**: Individual users earning UBA and attribution
- **Professional**: Enhanced analytics and business features
- **Organization**: Company-wide deployment and management
- **Academic**: Research institutions with special permissions

### **What You Own vs What UATP Owns**
- **You Own**: Your conversations, attribution rights, economic earnings
- **UATP Owns**: The infrastructure, algorithms, and aggregate analytics
- **Shared**: Anonymous usage patterns for system improvement

## B - Benefits & Basic Income

### **Universal Basic Attribution (UBA)**
Every verified human receives monthly payments from the global commons fund:

**How Much?**
- Starts small: $5-20/month initially
- Grows with adoption: Could reach $200-500/month at scale
- Based on total AI economic value: 15% of all AI usage goes to UBA

**Who Gets It?**
- Any verified human with a UATP account
- No geographic restrictions
- No employment requirements
- Automated monthly distribution

### **Attribution Earnings**
Beyond UBA, you earn when your contributions help AI:

**Direct Attribution** (High Confidence >80%):
- Full credit for your specific contributions
- Immediate payment when AI uses your input
- Ranges from cents to hundreds of dollars per usage

**Weighted Attribution** (Medium Confidence 50-80%):
- Partial credit shared among contributors
- Still meaningful earnings for good contributions

**Commons Contribution** (Low Confidence <50%):
- Goes to UBA fund, benefits everyone
- Your contribution still helps humanity

### **Earning Examples**
- **Student helping with homework**: $0.50-5 per conversation that influences AI
- **Professional providing expertise**: $10-100 per valuable insight
- **Creative work**: $1-50 per artistic contribution used by AI
- **Code contributions**: $5-500 per algorithm or solution

**Indirect Value Creation**:

- AI provider reports economic impact of AI usage
- UATP algorithms estimate total value generated
- Attribution confidence calculated for each contributor

**Step 2: Attribution Calculation**
```
Total Value Generated: $1000
├── 15% to UBA Fund: $150 (distributed to all humans)
├── High Confidence Attribution: $600 (direct to contributors)
├── Medium Confidence Attribution: $150 (weighted distribution)
└── Low Confidence: $100 (to commons fund)
```

**Step 3: Payment Distribution**
- Instant microtransactions for small amounts
- Monthly aggregated payments for regular contributors
- Annual distributions for UBA fund
- Bonus payments for exceptional contributions

## H - How It Works (Technical Overview)

### **The Big Picture**
UATP creates a "trust layer" between humans and AI that ensures fair attribution and prevents manipulation:

```
Human ↔ UATP Trust Layer ↔ AI System
   ↓           ↓              ↓
Contribution → Attribution → Economic Value
```

### **Core Technologies**

**Cryptographic Capsules**:
- Every AI interaction wrapped in tamper-proof "capsule"
- Ed25519 digital signatures prevent forgery
- Mathematical proof of what AI was thinking
- Audit trail for every decision

**Attribution Engine**:
- Semantic similarity analysis finds your contributions in AI responses
- Confidence scoring determines attribution strength
- Temporal tracking shows how ideas evolve over time
- Economic calculation converts attribution to payments

**Distributed Network**:
- No single point of failure or control
- Federation between different UATP nodes
- Cross-platform compatibility
- Quantum-resistant security

### **Step-by-Step Process**

**1. Conversation Capture**
- You talk to AI through UATP interface
- Your input gets cryptographically signed
- AI receives request with attribution metadata
- Response gets captured and analyzed

**2. Attribution Analysis**
- Semantic analysis compares AI response to your past contributions
- Machine learning models estimate your influence
- Confidence score calculated (0-100%)
- Attribution recorded in distributed ledger

**3. Economic Calculation**
- AI usage generates measurable economic value
- Your attribution percentage calculated
- Payment amount determined based on value and confidence
- Transaction recorded for transparency

**4. Distribution**
- Payments aggregated and processed
- UBA fund distributions calculated
- Your earnings deposited to chosen payment method
- Full audit trail maintained

### **Quality Assurance**
- **Peer Review**: Community validates attribution claims
- **Algorithmic Verification**: Multiple AI systems cross-check results
- **Economic Incentives**: False claims cost money, accurate claims earn money
- **Reputation Systems**: Long-term trust building through consistent quality

## L - Local vs Cloud Storage

### **Storage Architecture Overview**

**Hybrid Approach**: UATP uses both local and cloud storage for optimal security, performance, and user control:

```
Your Device (Local) ← Sync → UATP Cloud ← Federation → Global Network
```

### **Local Storage (Your Device)**

**What's Stored Locally**:
- Your private conversations and drafts
- Personal analytics and insights
- Attribution records and earnings history
- Encryption keys and security credentials
- Offline conversation backups

**Advantages**:
- **Complete Control**: You own the hardware and data
- **Privacy**: No one else can access without your permission
- **Performance**: Instant access, no network delays
- **Offline Access**: Works without internet connection
- **Security**: Air-gapped from external threats

**Local Storage Options**:
- **Mobile App**: 1-5GB of conversation history
- **Desktop Application**: 10-100GB of full archive
- **Local Server**: Unlimited storage for power users
- **External Drives**: Backup and archive solutions

### **Cloud Storage (UATP Network)**

**What's Stored in Cloud**:
- Anonymized conversation patterns
- Cross-platform attribution data
- Global knowledge graph connections
- Backup copies (encrypted with your keys)
- Sync data between your devices

**Advantages**:
- **Backup Protection**: Data safe even if device lost
- **Cross-Platform Sync**: Access from any device
- **Network Effects**: Your data helps improve global attribution
- **Scalability**: Handles massive computation for attribution
- **Federation**: Works across different UATP implementations

**Cloud Security**:
- **Zero-Knowledge**: Cloud never sees unencrypted data
- **Your Keys**: Only you can decrypt your information
- **Distributed**: Data spread across multiple secure locations
- **Auditable**: Full transparency in data handling

### **Storage Configuration Options**

**Option 1: Cloud-First (Recommended for Beginners)**
- Primary storage in UATP cloud
- Local caching for performance
- Automatic backup and sync
- Easy setup and maintenance
- Best for: Casual users, mobile-first

- Primary storage on your devices
- Selective cloud sync for attribution
- Maximum privacy and control
- Requires technical knowledge
- Best for: Privacy advocates, technical users

**Option 3: Hybrid (Balanced)**
- Important data stored locally
- Attribution data in cloud
- Configurable sync policies
- Balance of privacy and convenience
- Best for: Most users

**Option 4: Enterprise (Corporate)**
- Company-controlled storage
- Integration with corporate systems
- Compliance and governance features
- Professional support
- Best for: Organizations

## U - Universal Basic Attribution

### **What is UBA?**

Universal Basic Attribution ensures every verified human receives monthly payments from the AI economy, recognizing that all AI development builds on collective human knowledge and culture.

**Core Principle**: No matter how attribution algorithms work, everyone deserves to benefit from AI progress because AI is trained on the full spectrum of human knowledge, culture, and wisdom.

### **How UBA Works**

**Revenue Collection**:
- 15% of all AI economic value goes to UBA fund
- Collected from AI providers, not individual users
- Automatic collection through UATP infrastructure
- Transparent accounting and distribution

**Distribution Calculation**:
```
Total UBA Fund = 15% of global AI economic value
Monthly UBA per person = Total UBA Fund / Number of verified humans / 12 months
```

**Payment Process**:
- Monthly distributions on fixed schedule
- Automatic payment to preferred method
- No application or means testing required
- Global access regardless of location

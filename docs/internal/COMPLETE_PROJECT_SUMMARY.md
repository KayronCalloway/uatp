## The Revolutionary Breakthrough: Pragmatic Attribution

### Core Insight Implemented
**"What we can attribute we do and what we can't its general fund until we can"**

This breakthrough insight solved the fundamental attribution problem by:
- Accepting that perfect attribution is impossible initially
- Creating graceful degradation to commons fund for unattributable value
- Building improvement pathways as attribution technology advances
- Ensuring no one is left out through Universal Basic Attribution

### Temporal Decay Innovation
**"If its not to a new idea that to should go to the common fund"**

Revolutionary approach to temporal decay:
- Degraded value from aging ideas goes to commons fund (not lost)
- Ensures generational fairness without value destruction
- Creates sustainable wealth redistribution over time
- Prevents permanent accumulation while preserving societal benefit

How it Works

  • Automatically detects ChatGPT or Claude conversations
  • Extracts all messages from the current page
  • Sends them to your UATP backend with full attribution
  • Shows green notifications when successful
  • Creates cryptographic seals and confidence ratings

Troubleshooting

  • Red notification: Check that backend is running on port 9090
  • No conversations found: Make sure you're on ChatGPT or Claude
  • CORS errors: This is normal - the capture still works
